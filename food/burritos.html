Beef and Bean Green Chili Burritos

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (16 ounces) refried beans
  • 8 flour tortillas (6 inches) warmed
  • 1/2 pound ground beef, cooked and drained
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 can (4 ounces) chopped green chilies

Recipe:

  1. Spread refried beans over tortillas. Top each with beef and 2 tablespoons of cheese. Fold ends and sides over filling and roll up; place seam side down in a greased 13x9-in. baking dish. Sprinkle with chilies and remaining cheese.
  2. Bake, uncovered, at 350Â° for 20 minutes or until heated through. Yield: 4 servings.
