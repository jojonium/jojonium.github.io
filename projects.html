HCI PROJECT 5

HCI Project 5 is a group project for CS 3041, Human Computer Interactions. The project was used to explore the technology of affective computing, using technology to promote health and wellbeing. We used Affectiva's emotion-detecting API. The program analyzes your expression through your webcam as it shows you ten images of food, to see which one you like the most. Then it gives you a recipe for how to make that particular dish. To see how it works, click here.
